LUSINE Furniture Category Page Template

A refined, French-language e-commerce template for modular sofas, divans and chairs, featuring a promo-led grid, rich imagery and social proof.

Key features
Hero section with category title, subtitle and filter pills (type, color)
Responsive product grid with prices, badges (Nouveau, Solde) and color variants
Integrated promo banners (“Magasinez Delano”, cushions, Austin) in card and widescreen formats
Customer reviews carousel with photos, 5-star ratings and quotes
Branded header and footer with navigation, language selector, help, about and newsletter signup
Use cases
Furniture and interior brands launching a modular seating collection
Design-focused online shops needing a category layout that blends commerce, brand storytelling and social proof.
