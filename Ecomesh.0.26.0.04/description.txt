Urban Performance Lab E-commerce Landing Page Template

A sleek, editorial-style e-commerce landing page template for curated athletic footwear and apparel reviews, guides, and affiliate product recommendations.

Urban Performance Lab is a modern landing page template for curated athletic footwear and apparel, optimized for affiliate and review-driven brands.

What's Included
Hero section with bold imagery and dual CTAs
Curated collections grid (Running, Lifestyle, Training)
Product cards for affiliate e-commerce with pricing and outbound CTAs
Featured in-depth review module with ratings bars
Blog-style guides section for style and performance content
About and footer with social links, navigation, and legal copy
Key Features
Clean, editorial aesthetic with large type
Affiliate-ready product layouts ("Buy at" links)
Review-focused design with scores and highlights
Content hubs for guides and news
Responsive Tailwind CSS structure for easy customization
Primary use case: launch a brand-forward sneaker or performance gear review hub that blends e-commerce, editorial content, and affiliate monetization in one cohesive template.
